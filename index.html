Welcome to Risesi Salon Operators National Association / SACCO

Securing the future for the salon operators in kampala metropolitan women & youth 2022 – 2026

Amazing Services & Features for you

To grow the salon operator’s association with fully paid-up membership into a financially empowered National RISESI Umbrella Enterprise with 300,000 registered members (6,000 groups) in Uganda by 2026.

RISESI Skills Development Programme for Job Creation with Hands-on practical skills training in beautician, hair dressing, cosmetology, and Cosmetic formulas for industrial production and provision of startup Toolkits.

Established Financing Investment Vehicle RISESI SACCO with a Stimulus Rescue Revolving Fund for 20,000 salon operators to access credit for capitalization & expansion of their 12,000 businesses by 2026.

To establish of small scale manufacturing center with 4 branches in Uganda in promotion of youth inclusion in import substitution (NDPIII).

About What We Do & Who We Are
